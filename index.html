About nc yoga

I love yoga!

My very first experience with yoga was in a restorative yoga class in February 2013.

I started doing yoga regularly from September 2013 out of curiosity. It unexpectedly healed a painful lower back injury which I never had diagnosed or treated, after a few weeks.

I have tried many styles, including:

  • Ashtanga
  • Mysore
  • Vinyasa
  • Iyengar
  • Hatha
  • Restorative
  • Kundalini
  • Sivananda
  • Bikram
  • Hot yoga

I have been focusing primarily on ashtanga vinyasa yoga since January 2014.

I am an Yoga Alliance RYT 200 yoga teacher trainee, scheduled to complete in July 2016.

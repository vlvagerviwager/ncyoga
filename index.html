Hi! My name is Nikita, and I’m a Yoga Alliance RYT 250 Yoga Teacher based in Dublin, Ireland.

I teach Ashtanga, Vinyasa, and Hatha Yoga at all levels.

I’m available to teach studio, private, corporate, cover classes, and events.

My core belief as a teacher is that everyone should be able to do yoga - regardless of fitness level, body type, strength, or condition.

My classes focus on building strength in the body while staying light and comfortable, with correct alignment. I enjoy creating dynamic sequences and cooling down with supported restorative poses. I love teaching inversions and watching students surprise themselves with their courage and inner strength. I practice Ashtanga, Vinyasa, and Iyengar yoga and like to incorporate elements from all three into my classes.

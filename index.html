I’m a Yoga Teacher based in Dublin, Ireland.

I teach all levels of Ashtanga, Vinyasa, and Hatha Yoga.

My core belief as a teacher is that everyone should be able to do yoga - regardless of fitness level, body type, strength, or condition.

I’m available for studio classes, private, corporate, and cover. I teach at Samadhi Yoga Studios Dublin and Morning Gloryville events.

About me

I love yoga!

My very first experience with yoga was in a restorative yoga class in February 2013.

I started doing yoga regularly from September 2013 out of curiosity. It unexpectedly healed a painful lower back injury which I never had diagnosed or treated, after a few weeks.

I have practiced many styles of yoga, including Ashtanga, Mysore-style, Vinyasa, Iyengar, Hatha, Restorative, Kundalini, Sivananda, Bikram, and hot yoga. I have practiced in studios all over Dublin, and in America.

I mainly practice ashtanga vinyasa yoga and vinyasa yoga.

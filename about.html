About me

I love yoga!

My very first experience with yoga was in a restorative yoga class in February 2013.

I started doing yoga regularly from September 2013 out of curiosity. It unexpectedly healed a painful lower back injury.

Since then, yoga has completely changed my quality of life, and my passion is to share the same positive benefits I've experienced with the world. My training in Samadhi Dublin was based on classical yoga and includes anatomy and physiology, asana teaching and adjustments, pranayama, yoga history and philosophy, mantras, meditation, and kriyas. My classes include strengthening poses with a focus on correct alignment and safety, and I enjoy creating varied sequences and cooling down with restorative poses. I love teaching inversions and watching students surprise themselves with their courage and inner strength. I'm inspired by ashtanga, vinyasa, and Iyengar yoga.

I have practiced many styles of yoga, including Ashtanga, Mysore-style, Vinyasa, Iyengar, Hatha, Restorative, Yin, Kundalini, Sivananda, Bikram, and hot yoga. I have practiced in studios all over Dublin, and in the US. I attend a variety of workshops to deepen my practice and understanding of yoga, including sound meditation, mantras, etc. I continuously search and engage in ways to advance my practice, my teaching skills, my understanding of yoga including history and philosophy, and compassion.

My inspirations are Kino MacGregor, Laruga Glaser, Dylan Werner, and every teacher I've had the good fortune to learn from along my ongoing journey. Atma namaste!
